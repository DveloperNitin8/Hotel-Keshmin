About Hotel Keshmin

Your sanctuary of comfort in the heart of Bargarh.

Our Story

Hotel Keshmin has been a staple of Bargarh hospitality, offering a relaxing and comfortable stay for travelers. Located perfectly near Panichhatra Overbridge, we provide convenient access to the city while remaining a peaceful coastal retreat.

Our commitment is simple: creating an experience that feels like home. We focus on modern amenities, impeccable cleanliness, and a warm team dedicated to anticipating your every need.

Whether you're visiting for an important business trip, a family vacation, or just passing through the beautiful region of Odisha, we ensure your stay is completely flawless from check-in to check-out.
